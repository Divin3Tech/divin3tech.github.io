How It Works

Our Process

A systematic approach to get you from current state to audit-ready & compliant efficiently.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We start by understanding your business context, systems architecture, and compliance goals. This ensures the assessment is tailored to your actual needs—not generic templates.

02

Comprehensive Assessment

Thorough evaluation through documentation review, stakeholder interviews, and technical analysis. We assess controls, identify gaps, and understand your current security maturity.

03

Gap Analysis & Prioritization

Detailed mapping of gaps with risk-based prioritization. We distinguish between critical security issues requiring immediate attention and lower-priority documentation needs.

04

Remediation Roadmap

Phased implementation plan with clear timelines, resource requirements, and cost estimates. You'll know exactly what needs to happen and when.

05

Executive Presentation

We deliver findings through professional report and executive presentation. Clear communication ensures leadership understands risks, priorities, and path forward.

Case Studies

Client Success Stories

Real results from real engagements. Here's how we have helped companies achieve certification.

SOC 2 Type II SaaS

50-Person SaaS Startup

Challenge

Enterprise customers requiring SOC 2 Type II compliance. Company had minimal security documentation and informal processes.

Solution

Comprehensive readiness assessment identifying 38 gaps. Built 6-month implementation roadmap with phased approach. Provided ongoing advisory during remediation.

Result

Passed Type II audit with zero findings. Secured $2M enterprise contract within 2 months of certification.

ISO 27001 Fast-Track FinTech

FinTech Scale-Up

Challenge

Rapid growth company needed ISO 27001 for international expansion into EU market. Tight 8-month timeline.

Solution

Gap assessment revealed 45 gaps across all Annex A controls. Prioritized critical security controls first, then documentation and processes.

Result

Achieved certification in 7 months. Company successfully launched in Germany and UK markets on schedule.

ISO 27001 SOC 2 Healthcare

HealthTech Platform

Challenge

Healthcare customers requiring both ISO 27001 and SOC 2 compliance. Budget constraints limiting ability to pursue separately.

Solution

Integrated assessment leveraging control overlap between frameworks. Single remediation effort satisfying both certifications simultaneously.

Result

Achieved both certifications. 30% cost savings versus separate compliance efforts. Expanded into enterprise healthcare market.

CMMC 2.0 NIST 800-171 Defense

Solution
